We use your information to verify your identity, administer your membership as Investor-User, complete your transactions and administer your quotes/inquiries/grievances. We also use your Email address to communicate Notifications or other information that may be of interest or importance to you.

Participant Companies:

Our primary goal in collecting or demanding exhaustive information is to provide and improve information exchange on GREX Platform between the various Users and Participants through the various features as enabled for each participant which may change from time to time and thereby enable the Users to easily navigate the Site, develop interest and also make informed decisions for investments where possible.
