Sponsor

A sponsor is a legal entity (company or LLP) that handholds the companies to make them capital ready. Sponsors are grass root players, having helped companies raise capital. They also bring deep financial knowledge along with other statutory capabilities.
A sponsor ensures that right companies come on platform and their information is continuously updated on the platform. It also ensures that the best corporate governance practices are followed at companies, apart from managing investors of its portfolio companies.
