Events

  • DirectEXCHANGE

Direct EXCHANGE is the GREX outreach programme that will help start-ups understand the idea of GREX, how does it work, and how start-ups can leverage it.

This will be an interactive session where start-ups can question and clarify their queries directly from the leadership at GREX. This is NOT a product demonstration or a motivational speaker session. It's an informal meeting to facilitate a conversation and idea exchange between entrepreneurs, all over high tea!
